Using bitcoin as money

Bitcoin is just a ledger of transactions between participants on the network. Bitcoin was designed to be used as money, but a bitcoin transaction and the bitcoin network is independent from existing money and our financial and banking systems. When Andreas and Barry would like to conduct a transaction on the Bitcoin network, Andreas sends bitcoin from his address to Barry’s address, and the transaction is denominated in bitcoin units (BTC).

Bitcoin is software

Bitcoin is a piece of software which monitors and enables transactions between people who use the Bitcoin network. Participants of the network transact bitcoin with one another. The software allows the creation of a maximum of 21 million bitcoin, which are divisible to be more practical to use. The software runs simultaneously on the thousands or millions of computers of people who choose to participate in this network. The Bitcoin software is uniquely able to establish consensus between people using the network, without requiring trust.
