Bitcoin 101

February 1, 2022

Bitcoin is a piece of software which monitors and enables transactions between people who use the Bitcoin network.

Participants of the network transact bitcoin with one another. The software allows the creation of a maximum of 21 million bitcoin, which are divisible to be more practical to use.

The software runs simultaneously on the thousands or millions of computers of people who choose to participate in this network. The Bitcoin software is uniquely able to establish consensus between people using the network, without requiring trust.

Humans' desires to transact facilitated the emergence of money, a technology with a long history of development. Bitcoin is the latest of these technological developments.

All other narratives surrounding the value of bitcoin and the values of bitcoin are downstream to these basic features.
