Using bitcoin as money

February 1, 2022

Bitcoin is just a ledger of transactions between participants on the network.

Bitcoin was designed to be used as money, but a bitcoin transaction and the bitcoin network is independent from existing money and our financial and banking systems.

When Andreas and Barry would like to conduct a transaction on the Bitcoin network, Andreas sends bitcoin from his address to Barry’s address, and the transaction is denominated in bitcoin units (BTC).

What bitcoin is “worth” is determined by individuals, groups & society based on speculative demand (using bitcoin to store value into the future) and utility demand (using bitcoin to make a transaction on the network). This is similar to how gold has and has had value for thousands of years.

Because bitcoin is independent of existing monetary and financial networks, it is possible to use bitcoin anywhere in the world with internet. When you see the “price” of bitcoin, that’s the most up to date estimate that someone, somewhere in the world, is willing to pay for your bitcoin.
